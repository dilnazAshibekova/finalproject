Beverages of Kazakh cuisine

Kumys

a fermented dairy product traditionally made from mare's milk

Shubat

beverage of fermented camel milk, sparkling white with a sour flavor

Ayran

It is prepared from almost all types of milk which is put through a boiling process

Tea

an aromatic beverage prepared by pouring hot or boiling water

Katyk

It is produced from natural milk by fermenting it with special bacterial cultures.

Wheat koje

Boil wheat in milk and mix it with corn and raisins.

Kymyran

a drink usually made from a mixture of boiled water and milk.

Saumal

Powdered mare's milk. Used as a treatment for internal diseases
